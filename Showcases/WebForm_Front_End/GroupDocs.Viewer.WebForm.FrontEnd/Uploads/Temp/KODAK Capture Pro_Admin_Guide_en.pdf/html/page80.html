Setting up your Print  
options  
The Print option allows you to print your output.  
1
. Click Print on the Job Setup - Output tab.  
2
3
. Select the desired printer from the Printer name drop-down box.  
. If you want to change the printer properties, click Properties. The  
properties for the selected printer will be displayed.  
4
5
. Change the printer properties as desired and click OK.  
. Select the number of copies you want to print from the drop-down list and  
click OK.
